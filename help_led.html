  • Download application
  1. Apple users can search “MangoHome” in “APP store”to download and install; Android users can search “MangoHome” in “Google Play Store” to download and install. The APP is free to download and use.

Scan the QR code to download the APP

  • Directly connected to the device control
  1. After the LED strip is plugged in and indicator light turns on，search and connect the hotspot with cellphone.(WIFI hotspot ：Mangoxxxxxxxxxxxx).
  2. Open the APP and click "Connect the device directly for control" and you can directly control it.
  • Configure intelligent LED controller connected to the network
  1. After the LED strip is plugged in and indicator light turns on，search and connect the hotspot with cellphone.(WIFI hotspot ：Mangoxxxxxxxxxxxx).
  2. Open MangoHome APP, click “settings”, then enter select WiFi interface, and then enter your own WiFi name and enter the correct WiFi password, click “connect”.
Configuring  ：   White breathing light
Configuration success:   Green light last for 1s
Configuration failure:   Red light strobes on for 1s

*Unbound devices can only be controlled if the phone is connected to the router to which the device is bound.

  • Add timer
  1. Single : Only once is valid, it needs to be reset after it takes effect.
  2. Every day : effective daily, don' t need to setting.

*The controller timing function will fail after power off

  • Register and bind the device to my account
  1. Click “Register”, enter the registration page to enter the user's email address and password， then click “Register” to complete the registration, and then return to the login screen to log in.
  2. You can also use a third party account to log in, directly click on the following WeChat or Facebook icons.
  3. Enter into “My device” page after login success, long press “device name” and then click “bind to my Mango cloud” to bind the device. You can also modify the device name, restore the factory settings, unbound, delete devices and other operations.
  • How to set up with smart speakers
  1. Open MangoHome APP, enter “my device” surface, modify any device name to an customize name (like bedroom light) and bind the device to “my Mango cloud”.
  2. Open the APP of the smart speaker that you are going to access, search the skill named “MangoHome” from Amazon Alexa/TmallGenie , search the skill named “SmartMango” from GoogleHome.
  3. According to the operating instructions for account binding, after the binding is completed ,enter the device binding account and password, then click “authorization”. If you use a Wechat or Facebook account to bind the device, please select the Wechat or Facebook account for login.
  4. After the account is successfully bound and the Mango device is found, voice control can be performed. For example, say to Alexa:”Alexa,turn on the bedroom light”.

*MangoHome intelligent controller is compatible with the access control of three smart speakers ( GoogleHome, Amazon Alexa, Tmall Genis) . Before using a smart speaker to control the device, you must ensure that the controlled device had been bound to mango cloud.

  • Light dance with music
  1. Open MangoHome app, select a LED strip, enter the control interface, click "music" to enter music mode, then click "choose a music" to choose your favorite music to play and you can freely switch the different music and lighting effects in play.
  2. Microphone effect: Light effects change with the loudness and frequency of the user's voice.
  • How to restore factory setup
  1. Press and hold the controller button for at least 10 seconds until the white light turns on and then disconnect the power adapter. After power is on again, you can restore the factory configuration.
  • Safety Precautions
  1. Avoid use in humid and hot environments.
  2. When configuring network, please keep as close to the router as possible to avoid unsuccessful configuration due to weak WiFi signal. (Make sure the network is accessible).
